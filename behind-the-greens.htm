Between the Greens

Chikkanekkundi, off Whitefield, Varthur, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560087

Set across 9 acres off Varthur Road, Between the Greens by 42 Estates and SPD Developers is home to 147 premium 4 BHK villas built around light, air, and open greens. Every villa opens to courtyards and backyards that blend sunlight, air, and greenery into daily life. With nature all around, each day feels lighter, quieter, and deeply connected. It’s a home that truly lives Between the Greens.
